12   ## Context: 
13   - Customer Personality Analysis is a detailed analysis of a company’s ideal customers. It helps a business to better understand its customers and makes it easier for them to modify products according to the specific needs, behaviors and concerns of different types of customers. 
14   - Customer personality analysis helps a business to modify its product based on its target customers from different types of customer segments. For example, instead of spending money to market a new product to every customer in the company’s database, a company can analyze which customer segment is most likely to buy the product and then market the product only on that particular segment.

19   ### Attribute Information: 
20   - ID: Customer's unique identifier 
21   - Year_Birth: Customer's birth year 
22   - Education: Customer's education level 
23   - Marital_Status: Customer's marital status 
24   - Income: Customer's yearly household income 
25   - Kidhome: Number of children in customer's household 
26   - Teenhome: Number of teenagers in customer's household 
27   - Dt_Customer: Date of customer's enrollment with the company 
28   - Recency: Number of days since customer's last purchase 
29   - Complain: 1 if the customer complained in the last 2 years, 0 otherwise 
30   - MntWines: Amount spent on wine in last 2 years 
31   - MntFruits: Amount spent on fruits in last 2 years 
32   - MntMeatProducts: Amount spent on meat in last 2 years 
33   - MntFishProducts: Amount spent on fish in last 2 years 
34   - MntSweetProducts: Amount spent on sweets in last 2 years 
35   - MntGoldProds: Amount spent on gold in last 2 years 
36   - NumDealsPurchases: Number of purchases made with a discount 
37   - AcceptedCmp1: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the 1st campaign, 0 otherwise 
38   - AcceptedCmp2: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the 2nd campaign, 0 otherwise 
39   - AcceptedCmp3: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the 3rd campaign, 0 otherwise 
40   - AcceptedCmp4: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the 4th campaign, 0 otherwise 
41   - AcceptedCmp5: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the 5th campaign, 0 otherwise 
42   - Response: 1 if customer accepted the offer in the last campaign, 0 otherwise 
43   - NumWebPurchases: Number of purchases made through the company’s website 
44   - NumCatalogPurchases: Number of purchases made using a catalogue 
45   - NumStorePurchases: Number of purchases made directly in stores 
46   - NumWebVisitsMonth: Number of visits to company’s website in the last month
